PORTLAND, OREGON

THINGS TO DO

WHERE TO GO

Portland has loads of things to do. Right in town is Forest Park for a quick hike. Just over hour west is the coast for you to dip your feet in the Pacific. In the other direction, Mt Hood is just an hour away. Listed below are a few of our favorites places to go in town.

  • FOREST PARK

    Forest park is one of the largest urban forests in the country and has over 80 miles of trails to explore.

  • MT. TABOR

    In the middle of East Portland is Mt Tabor. Its an extinct volcano with sweeping views of the city and great trails for some light hiking.

  • SAUVIE ISLAND

    A large island in the middle of the Columbia River rich with farms to go berry picking, and public beaches to lounge on.

Portland is a foodie town, no doubt about it. There are too many options to list, however below are some of our favorites.

  • HEART ROASTERS

    The first cafe we went to when we moved to town, and our all-time favorite still.

  • BEULAHLAND

    Great neighborhood bar. This is where we planned a good portion of our wedding.

  • PAMBICHE

    Really tasty Cuban food.

  • PAYDIRT

    Our favorite whiskey bar connected to an incredible food court! We recommend the Old-Fashioned Roulette.

  • EGYPTIAN BROS

    Our favorite food cart in town. Get the lamb gyro!
