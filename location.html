Venue

We are having our ceremony and reception at Opal 28 here in Portland, Oregon. Found in the heart of east Portland and just blocks away from where we live. Opal 28 was originally built as a house in 1908, and in the 50s was converted into 4 one-bedroom apartments. The downstairs and patio are where the ceremony and reception will be held.

We want to welcome everyone to our neighborhood and take a glimpse into our lives.


Lodging

We encourage everyone to find housing near by the venue for ease of transportation. AirBnb Is a great option with plenty of listings in the neighborhood. Below are some cool and fun hotel options near by.

Jupiter Hotel
The Jupiter Hotel is a modern and hip hotel about one mile away from the venue.

Tiny Digs Hotel
If you are feeling a bit more adventerous, you can stay in a tiny home just a block away.

Lolo Pass Hotel
The Lolo Pass is hostel style lodging with private room accomodations less than one mile away.
